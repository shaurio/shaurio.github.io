October 2024 🏫📖🧮

It is now october, first month of school is over, I mean first month of highschool. Ha, it's crazy, I'm already in highschool, I have so many regrets. Though I could've had more, I will work on not having regrets, moving on with my mistakes.

October 1st​

The start of october, I mainly worked on my project for geography. I am happy with it, I don't regret doing it. I'm getting better at soccer, not bad, not bad at all. Today I ate delicious butter paneer my mom made, my gosh was it good.

October 2nd​

Yep, oct 2nd already. Last week my science teacher taught us how to read the perioidic table, it was mindblowing. I can mow draw out what the atomic structure of an atom looks like, that is on the perioidic table.

Now 6:00, man I've been wasting time, I feel horrible. Just really perturbed, filled with regret, I feel terrible. Ahhh... Dang it, I speak so boldly of not doing things you would regret, but I've made mistakes. It's okay, I am better for it, I simply will be better; I have to be.

October 3rd​

I woke up feeling great, if your not something, fake it till you make it, cause then it isn't faking at all.

I got ready for my field trip, to the farmers market, there are farmers market everywhere. This one was particulary large, and cool.

I kickscooted to school, went to class, when it was my 2nd period class I left and put my chromebook in my locker, so I dont lug it around during the trip. I boarded the bus, and drove the the market. There are lot's of wierd people, so choose who you want to be friends with. Are they worthy of being your friend? This only applies if your strong (confidence, power, muscle, intellect) enough to hold (gain, make them go to you) their friendship.

I look for people I want to ebe friends with, genuine people who are not stupid. This is why everyday is a fun day, and everyday is worthwhile, as I gain meaningful things with meaningfuly thinking people.

Anyways, once there we were told to stay in groups, I was with Conner, Ayaan, Abihjot, Shaurya. We had an assignment to complete, it was to ask the shopkeepers and vendors at the farmers market about their business, like how did they make it, what did they do to make it succesful, and what not. We first finished the assignment, (got there at 10:45) it was around 11 or so when we finished finished. We bought churros in celebration, it was a massive place. Anyways, lunch rolled around and we had lunch, yes. My friend bought 2 small (not worth it) slices of pizza, and I bought a poutine that was 8 in by 6in large. They cost the same, but my poutine was way bigger and way more filling. It was like a 3 course meal, so much food, it cost the same as the two slices of pizza.

Anyways, I had fun, bought some things, ate some mroe things, and just had an amazing time. That was truely awsome, I handled myself really well in conversations.

My teacher at school has a 150lbs one, so I asked to try, it's kinda easy. But the 150lbs one I have is hard, so hard I struggle to close it. Anyways, I will keep working on my business project.
